            <p>Foreign trade clothing refers to the domestic clothing manufacturers according to the samples from abroad or from abroad, domestic material processing and then shipped to foreign customers. The remaining part of the product after the completion of the order is called the foreign trade balance. Foreign trade clothing because it is exported abroad, so the implementation of standards are correspondingly stricter than domestic, so the quality is higher than similar products, resulting in a considerable part of the foreign trade tail has become the object of the majority of consumers.</p><p><br></p><p>This kind of clothing style specifications are often in accordance with the preferences of Europeans and Americans or Japan, South Korea, its style is often ahead of the eyes of domestic consumers, specifications are not necessarily in line with the characteristics of the Chinese body, but because of its unique appearance, good fabrics, fine workmanship, relatively low prices are still loved by many domestic consumers.</p><p><br></p><p>&quot;The real foreign trade clothing generally comes from the export clothing production enterprises.&quot; A boss familiar with foreign trade clothing business told Xinxin that foreign trade clothing style design is mostly provided by customers, and fabrics are purchased at home or imported from abroad according to customer requirements. When the manufacturer is in production, there are often several more pieces than the number required by the customer to prevent the shortage of pieces caused by the quality problems of the clothing, but the number of clothes produced by this multi-processing will not be too much. More foreign trade clothing comes from the &quot;defective goods&quot; that have been eliminated after many strict inspections before export, and most of these clothes flow to foreign trade clothing stores. Generally, each style has only 2 to 3 pieces, or even one piece, no more than 5 pieces. There are very few things that collide with each other! Hehe... Occasionally there are more than 5 pieces, then there must be some small flaws that do not affect the dress.</p><p><br></p>
